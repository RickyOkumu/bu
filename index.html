Projects by Bunyala Agri climate impact solutions
Our Projects

At Bunyala Agri Climate Impact Solutions, we are committed to driving sustainable development and community resilience through climate action, smallholder farmer empowerment, and ecosystem restoration.Our work aligns with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly SDG 1 (No Poverty), SDG 2 (Zero Hunger), SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being), SDG 5 (Gender Equality), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption & Production), SDG 13 (Climate Action), and SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals).

BSF and tree planting renewable energy distribution to smallholder farmers in western Kenya Sample Projects by Bunyala Agri Climate Impact Solutions Black Soldier Fly (BSF) Technology for Sustainable Agriculture

  • 1
    Objective:
    To convert organic post-harvest waste into high-protein Black Soldier Fly (BSF) products for aquaculture and livestock farmers.
    Impact:
    Reduction of organic waste in the environment. Affordable and sustainable feed for fish, poultry, and livestock farmers. Promotion of circular economy practices in the agricultural sector. Community Tree Planting and Agroforestry Programs
  • 2
    Objective:
    To enhance biodiversity, combat deforestation, and build resilience to climate change through large-scale tree planting initiatives.
    Impact:
    Increased forest cover in Western Kenya. Improved soil fertility and reduced erosion. Carbon sequestration to mitigate climate change effects. Creation of agroforestry systems to boost livelihoods. Renewable Energy Distribution for Smallholder Farmers
  • 3
    Objective:
    To promote access to clean and affordable energy solutions such as solar panels, energy-efficient cookstoves, and biogas systems.
    Impact:
    Reduced dependency on traditional wood fuel, reducing deforestation. Improved air quality and reduced greenhouse gas emissions. Enhanced productivity by providing reliable energy for farming and household use. Climate-Smart Agriculture Training Programs
  • 4
    Objective:
    To train smallholder farmers in sustainable farming practices, water conservation, and climate adaptation techniques.
    Impact:
    Increased crop yields and food security. Improved water management and soil conservation practices. Empowered farmers with knowledge and tools to adapt to changing climate conditions. Youth and Women Empowerment through Green Jobs

Bunyala Agri Climate Impact Solutions

Our Services & Solutions

  • Training smallholder farmers in climate-resilient agricultural practices (agroecology, permaculture, regenerative farming). Promoting drought-resistant crops, soil conservation, and water harvesting techniques.

  • Distribution of solar-powered irrigation systems to reduce reliance on fossil fuels. Promotion of biogas technology for sustainable cooking and waste management. Piloting renewable energy-powered fish processing units to support fisher communities.

  • Tree and bamboo planting along riverbanks and dykes to prevent soil erosion and restore biodiversity. Waste management initiatives, including plastic recycling and circular economy models.

  • Development of the Smart Resilience Network for Disaster Management (SRNDM) to enhance climate adaptation. Deployment of IoT sensors and AI-powered early warning systems for flood-prone areas. Training in emergency preparedness, first aid, and risk mitigation strategies.

  • Strengthening local smallholder farmers’ and fisherfolk’s market access through digital platforms. Capacity building in financial literacy, cooperative management, and agribusiness development. Supporting women and youth entrepreneurs in accessing microfinance and business incubation programs. Partnering with Catalyst 2030, Kenya Climate Innovation Center (KCIC), and SHOFCO to scale impact.

  • Community-driven mangrove restoration for coastal protection. Waste management initiatives, including plastic recycling and circular economy models. Enhancing carbon sequestration efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
