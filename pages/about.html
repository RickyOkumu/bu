Welcome To Bunyala Agri-Climate Impact Solutions

Bunyala Agri-Climate Impact Solutions is a community-based organization in Busia County, Kenya, dedicated to empowering smallholder farmers and fisher communities to build resilience against climate change.

Our mission encompasses promoting sustainable farming practices, conserving natural ecosystems, and enhancing rural livelihoods to achieve sustainable food security.


Key Initiatives:

1. Climate-Smart Agriculture:
We provide training and resources to farmers on sustainable farming techniques, aiming to boost productivity while preserving natural ecosystems.

2. Waste-to-Value Innovations:
By converting organic waste into high-quality animal and fish feeds, we address food waste challenges and support sustainable agriculture.

3. Community Engagement:
Our programs emphasize equity, inclusion, and environmental justice, fostering collaboration among local stakeholders to drive positive change.

4. Climate Advocacy:
We invest in climate advocacy to improve the quality of life for rural households, aiming to reduce the impact of climate change on agricultural production.



Through these efforts, Bunyala Agri-Climate Impact Solutions strives to create sustainable, community-driven solutions that enhance the resilience and well-being of vulnerable populations in the face of climate challenges.
