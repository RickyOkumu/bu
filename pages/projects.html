Bunyala Agri Climate Impact Solutions

Our Projects

  • Training smallholder farmers in climate-resilient agricultural practices (agroecology, permaculture, regenerative farming). Promoting drought-resistant crops, soil conservation, and water harvesting techniques.

  • Distribution of solar-powered irrigation systems to reduce reliance on fossil fuels. Promotion of biogas technology for sustainable cooking and waste management. Piloting renewable energy-powered fish processing units to support fisher communities.

  • Tree and bamboo planting along riverbanks and dykes to prevent soil erosion and restore biodiversity. Waste management initiatives, including plastic recycling and circular economy models.

  • Development of the Smart Resilience Network for Disaster Management (SRNDM) to enhance climate adaptation. Deployment of IoT sensors and AI-powered early warning systems for flood-prone areas. Training in emergency preparedness, first aid, and risk mitigation strategies.

  • Strengthening local smallholder farmers’ and fisherfolk’s market access through digital platforms. Capacity building in financial literacy, cooperative management, and agribusiness development. Supporting women and youth entrepreneurs in accessing microfinance and business incubation programs. Partnering with Catalyst 2030, Kenya Climate Innovation Center (KCIC), and SHOFCO to scale impact.

  • Community-driven mangrove restoration for coastal protection. Waste management initiatives, including plastic recycling and circular economy models. Enhancing carbon sequestration efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
